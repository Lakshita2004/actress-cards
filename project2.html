Top 4 Actresses of Bollywood

Katrina kaif

Katrina Kaif (born July 16, 1983) is a prominent British-Indian actress and model known for her work in Bollywood films. Rising from a modeling career to become one of India's highest-paid actresses, she achieved fame with hits like Namastey London (2007) and Ek Tha Tiger (2012). She married actor Vicky Kaushal in 2021.

  • Action and Stunts
  • Modelling and Fashion

Kareena Kapoor Khan

Kareena Kapoor Khan (born 21 September 1980), also known by her nickname Bebo, is a leading Indian actress known for her roles in Bollywood films. Daughter of actors Randhir Kapoor and Babita, she debuted in Refugee (2000) and became a top actress with hits like Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham and Jab We Met. She married actor Saif Ali Khan in 2012.

  • Versatility
  • Production & New Genres
  • Dancing

Aishwarya Rai

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan (born 1 November 1973) is a renowned Indian actress, model, and the winner of the Miss World 1994 pageant. Widely regarded as one of the most popular and influential celebrities in India, she has established a successful career in both Hindi (Bollywood) and Tamil cinema, in addition to several international projects.

  • Philanthropy
  • Arts and Performance
  • Zoology and Science

Madhuri Dixit

Madhuri Dixit (born May 15, 1967) is an iconic Indian actress and trained Kathak dancer, renowned as one of Bollywood's leading stars of the 1980s, 1990s, and early 2000s. Debuting in Abodh (1984), she achieved stardom with Tezaab (1988) and won four Filmfare Best Actress awards, recognized for hits like Dil, Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!, and Dil To Pagal Hai.
